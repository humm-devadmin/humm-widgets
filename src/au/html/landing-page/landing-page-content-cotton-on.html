FAQs

What is humm?

Humm is a ‘Buy Now, Pay Later’ service that lets you purchase today and pay back over time. It’s the only 2 speed BNPL service that gives you a ‘Little things’ wallet for smaller purchases, and a “Big things’ wallet for life’s bigger buys. ‘Little things’ lets you pay over 10 or 20 weeks (that’s 2.5 or 5 months), and ‘Big things’ gives you more time to pay. It’s fast and easy to use, with an ever-increasing range of places to shop. Download the humm App or go to shophumm.com.au and be interest free forever - woohoo!

Who can humm?

To humm, you simply need to tick the following boxes:

- Be a permanent resident of Australia
- Be 18 years or older
- Have a valid email and mobile number
- Have an Australian debit or credit card
- Have an Australian Driver’s license, passport or Medicare card
- Have a permanent job (at least 25 hours per week) or be on an aged/veteran’s pension

How do I shop with humm?

Buying ‘Little things’ online:

Simply select humm at checkout and you will be taken through to the humm site to login or  sign up. Just follow the prompts and once you confirm your payment plan and pay your 20%  first payment, your purchase can be delivered. It’s fast and super simple to use no matter  where you shop.

Buying ‘Little things’ instore:

Simply log into the humm App and generate a barcode. Then, take this to the checkout with your purchase, and they will scan or input the code. Humm is fast and super simple to use, no matter where you shop.

With no interest, are fees high?

No, they’re not. When you buy using your ‘Little things’ wallet with humm, there are no fees, if you pay it back over 2.5 months (10 weeks). If you pay over 5 months (20 weeks), you’ll pay just $8 a month.

The good news is, you only ever pay a single $8 monthly fee, no matter how many humm payment plans you have. Sweet!
